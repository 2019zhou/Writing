### 常见错误
1. 逗号前后两个句子都有两套主谓宾
2. thus不可以链接两套主谓宾

Kargan maintains that an infant's reaction to its first stressful experiences are part of a natural process of development, not harbingers of childhood unhappiness or ____ signs of adolescent.    
选择prophetic    
or之后实际上少了not    
not A or B 既不是，　也不是     
would/ might/ tend to
